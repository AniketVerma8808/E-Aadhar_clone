Aadhar Card Update:

An aadhaar is a 12 digit unique number that has an individual's information such as name, date of birth, biometrics, phone number, address etc. If anyone wants to change their information on their aadhaar they can update aadhar card with the changed information. The aadhar card update process is quite simple, an individual can follow the steps mentioned below to update aadhar card.

Steps To Update Aadhar Card:

  1. Visit eaadhar card website and click on my aadhar update.
  2. There will be an option to update aadhaar at the enrollment/update centre, click on that.
  3. Locate an enrollment/aadhar centre page will be opened.
  4. Search the enrollment centre by entering your pin code and captcha.
  5. Visit the aadhar card update centre for the updating process.

Check Aadhar Card Update Status:

Once an individual has applied for the aadhar update, they can check whether the update has been completed or not. To check the update status visit our eadharcard.com website and follow the steps mentioned below:

  • Click on my aadhaar and choose the option from the update aadhar card column.
  • Choose to check aadhaar status.
  • A new page will be displayed that will ask you for the enrollment ID (Aadhar Card EID).
  • Enrollment Id will be present on the slip that you have got from the enrollment centre you visited for the update aadhaar process.
  • Enter the enrollment id, fill the captcha and click proceed.
  • Your aadhaar update status will be displayed on the screen.

How to Update eAadhar Card Online?

Steps to update eaadhar card online:

  • Open our website www.eadharcard.com.
  • On the home option, you will see my eaadhar update option click on that.
