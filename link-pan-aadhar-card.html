Steps to Link Pan Card with Aadhar Card:

By signing on to the income tax e-filing system, you may link your Aadhaar number to your PAN. On the income tax portal, there are two options.

  1. Without logging in to your account (6 steps)
  2. Logging the account (8 steps)
Method 1: Link Pan with Aadhar Number Without logging the Account:

Step 1: Go to www.incometax.gov.in for further information. When you get to the bottom of the main page, select the ‘Our Services' link.
Step 2: Enter the following details:

  • PAN Number
  • Aadhaar Number
  • Registered Name
  • Registered Mobile Number

Step 3: If your Aadhaar card only has your year of birth, tick the box that says “I have just year of birth in Aadhaar card”.
Step 4: Check the box that reads, "I accept to have my Aadhaar information validated." To continue with the Aadhaar connecting process, you must tick this box.
Step 5: Select Continue.
Step 6: Your mobile number will be sent a 6-digit Time Password. On your screen, enter this OTP on the verification page. Click the ‘Validate' button after entering the OTP.

Method 2: Link Pan with Aadhar Number With logging the Account:

Step 1: Sign up for the e-filing site if you haven't already.
Step 2: Enter the User ID to access the Income Tax Department's e-Filing portal.
Step 3: Secure access message confirmation and password entry. Click on "continue" to continue.
Step 4: Following your initial log in, select "Link Aadhaar". Other options include visiting My Profile and selecting 'Link Aadhaar' under the Personal Details section.
Step 5: When you register on the e-Filing site, your name, date of birth, and gender will already be filled in. As though you were using Aadhaar, enter your number and name. Your Aadhaar card's details should match those on the screen.
Step 6: By checking the box "I agree to authenticate my Aadhaar data", you must provide your approval.
Step 7: Click on the ‘Link Aadhaar’ button.
Step 8: If your Aadhaar number has been successfully connected to your PAN card, a pop-up notification will let you know.
