Eligibility Criteria for Uidai Exam:

Candidates who want to make their career as the operator or supervisor, or CELC operator should meet the qualification to apply for this exam. The candidates should ensure they qualify the minimum eligibility criteria in terms of educational qualification and age limit. The minimum age limit s the applicant should be 18 years old. The minimum qualification for the post of operator or CELC or supervisor is they should complete their 12th grade from a recognized board of India.

For CELC operators, you need to qualify only for your 10th:

  • The latest copy of e-Aadhaar, e-KYC file, and the share code.
