Uidai Exam Registration Form 2023 - Apply Uidai Exam Application Form & Fees

Uidai Exam Online Form:

The candidates who are willing to give the UIDAI exam need to know the upcoming notification of the exam for 2022. Do you want to know the upcoming and current notification of Uidai Exam Application Form, you can visit our official website.

To fill the registration form of UIDAI, the Candidate needs to learn the eligibility criteria, steps to fill the application form or UIDAI Exam Registration Procedure, and documents you need to submit. In this article, we are going to help all those candidates who are planning to apply for the upcoming exams or Uidai Recruitment 2022.

Steps to Apply for UIDAI Online form 2022:

To complete the Uidai Exam Online Form, what you have to do is:

  • Open our website eadharcard.com.
  • Go to the UIDAI section for UIDAI Exam Registration Procedure.
  • Complete the Uidai application form.
  • Upload the documents and required images.
  • Submit the Uidai Exam Online Form fee.
  • Ask for the Print out for further use

Step 1: In the initial stage of UIDAI Exam Registration Procedure, the Candidate first visits the official website and then opens and downloads the aadhar card and chooses the share code.

Step 2: By uploading the Aadhaar XML File, you can click on the new user. Now fill up your Share Code and Mobile Number as per the Aadhaar Card for Uidai Exam Online Form.

Step 3: Once the information submitted by you is verified, you will receive a one-time verification code on your mobile number for Uidai Exam Registration Form.

Step 4: Now, you have to put this OTP on the required box. After that, you will receive a Registration Id, and a Default Password on your mobile number will be shared by the authority of Uidai Exam Online Form. You have to change this Default Password when logging in for the first time.

Step 5: Once you submit this Registration Id and the changed password, you will see that all the aadhar details will be there in the form automatically. The only thing you have to submit is the Educational Qualifications, Enrolment Agency Code, Preferred Examination Centre, Certification Role, and that’s it. Now click on the Uidai Exam Registration Form submit button.

Step 6: Once you complete the UIDAI Exam Registration Procedure, what you have to do is submit the Application Fee. For this, you have to click on the “Payment Tab.”There you get two different options one is online payment, and another is fee already paid. If you are registering for this exam for the first time, then you have to click on the online payment. The Uidai Exam Online Form fee already paid is only applicable for the candidates who already paid their fee but didn’t have any scheduled exam for the 180 days.

Step 7: Once you complete the payment procedure of Uidai Exam Registration Form, the seat booking page will be activated on your screen. Now you can book your center for the exam as per your preferred location. You should attempt the Mock Tests to experience the actual Certification Exam.

Step 8: Once you complete all the above Uidai Exam Registration Form procedures, you will receive an expected date for admit card, which has all the details of the Candidate.

Important Guidelines you should follow for Uidai Registration form:

There are a few important things that you need to consider while completing the Uidai Exam Application Form process of the UIDAI exam registration form.

  1. You should remember the Registration Id and the new password which you set during the form fill up. You can also note it down on paper.
  2. Once you pay the amount, it will not refund you, no matter what the circumstance is. So, before submitting the amount, think twice to check the given information are correct or not.
  3. The Uidai Exam Registration Form fee for certification you paid will be only valid for 180 days from the date on which the payment was made.
  4. If you didn’t get your preferred location or Test Centre even after 15 days of Online Fee Payment, you need to write an email to the official mail id of Uidai Recruitment 2022 by submitting the information like registration number and proof of payment to us.

About UIDAI Exam Application Fees:

  • The Uidai Exam Application Form Fee is only 470 INR that you can pay via debit/credit card/net banking or Challan of SBI bank any branch. You have to pay the full amount inclusive of GST for successfully appearing in the UIDAI Exam.
  • Are you filling the retest form for Uidai Exam Registration Form? If yes, then you only have to pay INR 235.41 inclusive of GST.
  • The candidates of Uidai Recruitment 2022 who are either absent or failed in the exam can give the Retest of the UIDAI Exam.
  • There is no such limitation on the number of attempts for the UIDAI Exam. You can apply for the exam until you get a pass.

Required Document for UIDAI Exam Application Form:

Candidates should have the following Required Documents for Applying Uidai Exam Online form to complete the successful registration form of UIDAI.

  1. The number you submit in the form should be in working condition. Any notification for the exam you will get on this same number.
  2. The Candidate should only upload the Latest Photograph in his/her Aadhaar Card before downloading the E-Aadhaar XML for verification.
  3. In the test center, you should pick the latest copy of your e-Aadhaar Card with Latest Photograph and Admit Card for verification purposes of Uidai Recruitment 2022.
  4. In case if the Candidate cannot show the Admit Card at the time of verification and if the photograph doesn’t match, he/she will be immediately disqualified.
  5. The Candidate’s Aadhaar Number should not have been blacklisted by any agency.
  6. The Candidate must attain the minimum Education Criteria and other requirements required for the Role.
  7. The Candidate must check the information before making the final submission and go through the Uidai Recruitment 2022.
