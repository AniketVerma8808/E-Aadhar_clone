What is Aadhar Card Verification?

To activate the aadhaar card or aadhaar number, an individual needs to verify the existence of the card or number. The aadhar card verification is a process to activate an individual’s aadhaar card. The aadhaar card/ aadhaar has a unique 12 digit number which is known as an aadhaar number that has all the demographic and biometric information of an individual. The aadhaar card verification can be done through our website online.

How to Verify Aadhar Card Online?

We have tried to make the aadhaar verification process easy and simple, now you won’t need to step out from your house for the aadhaar verification. Just verify aadhar card online by following these steps mentioned below:

Steps to verify aadhar card online:

  • Visit the eadharcard.com website.
  • Open the home page, go to my aadhaar.
  • Choose aadhaar services.
  • A list will be displayed, choose to verify an aadhaar from there.
  • A fresh page will be opened which will ask for your 12 digit aadhaar number.
  • Enter the 12 digit aadhaar number, verify the captcha and submit.

Why Do We Need To Verify Aadhar Card?

An Aadhaar card is an ID which is used to verify the details about the cardholder. All the biometrics and demographic data are attached with the aadhaar card thus an individual who holds an aadhaar card can easily be verified. Aadhaar card is valid all over India as a verification ID. Thus it makes it very important for an individual to verify their aadhaar card to make it valid. Once the aadhaar card verification process is completed an individual should check the demographic details, so that if there is any mistake in the demographic information one can rectify it.

How to Check If my Aadhaar Card is Activated or Deactivated?

After the issue of aadhaar number or any update in aadhaar, an individual must check whether their aadhaar has been activated or not. For checking the status of aadhaar activation you won’t need to visit the aadhaar center or enrollment center. An individual can check the aadhaar activation status online through our website.

  1. Go to the website of the aadhaar card.
  2. Click on the aadhaar services.
  3. Click “Verify Aadhaar Number”.
  4. It will redirect you to a new page, enter your 12 digit aadhaar number there.
  5. Fill the Captcha.
  6. Enter the security code sent on your mobile number that is registered with your aadhaar number/aadhaar card.
  7. If there is a green tick mark then your aadhaar is verified and activated.
