What is an Aadhar Card

Aadhar card is an identity card issued by UIDAI on the behalf of the Indian government. Aadhaar card has a unique 12 digit number that is known as aadhar no, that number contains all your basic information. All the residents of India can enroll for the aadhaar no matter what age, caste, or religion they belong to. Aadhaar holds a person's basic information such as name, address, biometrics data, age, etc. An individual can apply for an aadhaar, The aadhaar number will be issued once you can reprint it or download it again but no new aadhaar number will be issued to the one who already had one.

How to Apply for New Aadhar Card?

To apply for a new aadhar is really simple. Aadhaar card is issued to the person who doesn’t have any aadhaar number previously, all the Indians are eligible to enroll for an aadhaar number. We have mentioned the steps to apply for a new aadhaar. An individual needs to go to the enrollment center to apply for a new aadhaar.

Steps to Apply New Aadhaar Card Online:

  • Collect all the documents required for applying the aadhaar.
  • Find an aadhaar enrollment centre near you on our website.
  • Visit the aadhaar enrollment centre with all your documents, fill the form there and submit it.
  • After submitting all your documents, submit your biometrics too.
  • You will get an enrollment slip that will contain a 14 digit number, with help of that you can track your aadhaar status.
  • Keep the aadhaar enrollment slip with you till you get your aadhaar card.
