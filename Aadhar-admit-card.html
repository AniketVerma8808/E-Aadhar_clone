Process of Uidai Exam Admit Card 2022:

Are you also a candidate for the UIDAI? If you are planning to give the examination in 2022 and have already filled the form, then here is the process to download the Uidai Exam Admit Card:

  1. In The first step, the Candidate has to download “Offline Aadhaar XML File” using the official link and share the code.
  2. After that, you have to become a “New User .”Now you need to upload the Aadhaar XML File along with the Mobile Number and share code. Make sure you submit the same number of mobile that is given in the aadhar card.
  3. Once the mobile number and your aadhar number are verified, you will receive the OTP on your mobile phone. The OTP will be sent to the Candidate’s Registered Mobile Number. This is sent for the verification of the Candidate.
  4. Now a default password will be sent to the Candidate’s Registered Mobile Number. The Registration Id of the Candidate is also shared by the authority and exam organizers. You need to change this default password with your password when you log in for the First Time.
  5. Once the Candidate login with the site using the registration number and Id, and the new password. When you open the form already, most of the information is filled in. The Candidate only has to enter his/her Educational Qualifications. Along with that, you need to fill the Enrolment Agency Code, Examination Centre, and Certification Role before submitting it.
  6. After you submit the UIDAI Application Form, you need to fill the examination amount after clicking on the “Payment Tab.” Now, after this, you will get the two options where one is Online Payment and Fee Already Paid. After making the successful payment process, the seat booking page will be activated. Now you can book your exam slot, test center and confirm seat booking.
  7. As soon as a person book the slot, the UIDAI card should all the details like the Exam Time, Test Centre, Important Points, Address of Test Centre, and Payment Details, etc. You should fulfill all the requirements that are provided by the authorities.
