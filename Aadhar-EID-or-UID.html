What is Aadhar EID or UID?

Aadhar EID:

Aadhar EID is a 14-digit enrollment number (1234/12345/12345) and a 14-digit time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm: ss) are shown on your enrolment or update acknowledgment slip. Your Enrolment ID is made up of 28 digits (EID).
